### Q2: upsert_settings
```python
pg_insert(NotificationSetting)
  .values(org_id, user_id, **data)
  .on_conflict_do_update(constraint="uq_notification_settings_org_user", set_=data)
  .returning(NotificationSetting)
```
- PostgreSQL UPSERT (ON CONFLICT): ✅
- 유니크 제약조건 활용: ✅
